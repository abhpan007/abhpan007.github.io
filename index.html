I’m Abhigyan (Abhi) Pandya, currently in the MSBA program at UT Austin. My work centers on practical ML and analytics—projects that align with business goals and get used. Highlights include: an AI travel recommender, an NLP pipeline over 30k BeerAdvocate reviews, CVaR portfolio optimization experiments, and a senior-care consulting tool that automated paper templates into a Spark-based system and won immediate adoption. I enjoy building the “last mile”—dashboards, lightweight apps, and write-ups that make models legible to non-technical teams. Outside of work I’m collecting passport stamps (and photos on @abhiroams), and playing tennis, golf, or pickleball—anything with a scoreboard and a little sunshine.
